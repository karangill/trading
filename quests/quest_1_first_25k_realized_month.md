## Plan

### Mission alignment

The Mission is to develop trading into a major wealth-compounding pillar — ultimately resolving whether systematic discretionary edge produces 7+ figure annual income. Quest 1 is the first concrete financial milestone toward that. It's the moment we transition from "we have a framework" to "we have a framework that produces measurable income."

Quest 1 isn't BX replacement. BX replacement is Quest 3 ($52k/month sustained). Quest 1 is the first proof-of-concept month: a single calendar month with ≥$25k realized P&L while operating the framework at high process compliance. Half-step toward BX-replacement viability.

### Success criteria

Both must be met:

1. **Financial:** ≥$25,000 realized P&L within a single calendar month
2. **Process:** ≥90% process compliance during that month (tickets complete, journals timely, rule violations minimized)

### Time guard

Review at 4 weeks elapsed (~2026-06-19) regardless of whether the financial criterion has been met. The 4-week mark is when we evaluate: are we on track, off-track, or did we hit it? Three possible outcomes at review:

- **Hit:** Quest 1 closes; Quest 2 opens
- **On-track but not yet:** Quest 1 continues; possibly extend Sprint 1 or open Sprint 2
- **Off-track:** Quest 1 restructured (different financial target) or Sprint 1 extended with revised framework

### Sizing context

Quest 1 starts at **Tier 1: Sprint start** sizing — 1.0% max premium per campaign, 0.50% standard. Tier 2 unlocks at ≥10 trades with ≥90% process compliance and positive aggregate R. See `Trading_OS.md` § 12.

### What success looks like

A realistic shape of success for Quest 1:

- ~15-25 trades over the 4-week window
- Mix of setup types A/B/C with selective D (Compounding Add) within winning campaigns
- 1-3 active focus tickers + SPY/QQQ per week
- Process compliance >90%, A-Trade rate ≥70%
- Realized P&L approaches or exceeds $25k
- No catastrophic loss, no drawdown >8%
- Framework v0.4 candidate changes identified for Sprint 2

### What failure looks like

- <10 trades by week 4 (universe too restrictive or signals not present)
- Process compliance <90% (framework too loose, or trader discipline breaking)
- Any catastrophic loss (single trade >2x planned, or drawdown >8%)
- Realized P&L significantly negative

Failure at week 4 isn't catastrophic — it's information. Sprint 1 extends or Sprint 2 opens with revised framework. Quest 1 may persist beyond 4 weeks if the financial target is approachable; Quest 1 restructures if not.
